Coffee Shake

Description

This is a blended-coffee treat much like an iced cappuccino you might buy at a restaurant. You can make it non-dairy by using soy milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon instant coffee granules
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar, or to taste
  • 6 ice cubes
  • 2 teaspoons chocolate syrup

Steps

  1. Combine the instant coffee, milk, vanilla extract, sugar, ice, and chocolate syrup in a blender; blend until smooth
